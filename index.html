A guide to your first ride

  • Get ready to sweat. Bring your water and towels.
  • Arrive 15 minutes early. We’ll get your bikes set up properly for your safety.
  • Wear light, breathable clothes and closed-toe athletic shoes for the ride.
  • Switch on and put on your headphones. Set your preferred volume.
  • Follow the music, but listen to your body. Go at your own pace, especially if it’s your first time riding.
  • Enjoy the ride.
